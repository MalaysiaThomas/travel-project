One Year In — So We Did It Big

To celebrate our first year together, we took off on a two-part adventure: first stop, Florida for theme parks and nightlife. From screaming on roller coasters to sipping cocktails at hidden speakeasies, it was non-stop fun.

Then, we set sail on a 7-day Caribbean cruise that took us to the Dominican Republic, Puerto Rico, and St. Thomas. Blue water, warm breezes, and unforgettable memories — this trip was everything we dreamed of and more.

This wasn’t just a vacation — it was a celebration of everything we’ve built together.
